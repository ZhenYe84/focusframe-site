Privacy Policy

FocusFrame — Last updated March 24, 2026

TL;DR

We do not collect any data. Period.

All of our apps process your data entirely on your device. Nothing is sent to us, to advertisers, or to any third party. We have no servers, no analytics, and no tracking.

Apps Covered by This Policy

This privacy policy applies to all apps published by FocusFrame:

SplitCheck PetLog FocusFrame VoiceInk PDF Toolbox PhotoBatch

Data Collection

Our apps do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected (name, email, phone, etc.)
  • No usage analytics or behavioral tracking of any kind
  • No advertising identifiers or ad networks
  • No third-party SDKs, services, or APIs
  • No cookies, fingerprinting, or device profiling
  • No account creation required to use any app

On-Device Processing

All data you create or import within our apps is processed and stored locally on your device. This includes:

  • SplitCheck — Bill calculations and split history stay on your device
  • PetLog — Pet health records and feeding logs stay on your device
  • FocusFrame — Tasks, focus sessions, and statistics stay on your device
  • VoiceInk — Voice recordings are transcribed on-device using Apple's Speech framework; audio is never uploaded
  • PDF Toolbox — PDF files are processed entirely on-device; no documents leave your device
  • PhotoBatch — Image processing happens on-device; your photos are never uploaded

iCloud Sync

Some of our apps offer optional iCloud sync to keep your data in sync across your Apple devices. When enabled:

  • Data is synced through your own iCloud account, managed by Apple
  • We have no access to your iCloud data
  • Sync is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time in your device's Settings

Notifications

Some apps (PetLog, FocusFrame) may request permission to send local notifications (reminders, timers). These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to any external server.

Focus Status & Live Activities

FocusFrame may request access to your Focus Status (via NSFocusStatusUsageDescription) to detect whether you have a Focus Mode enabled, allowing the app to intelligently manage timer behavior. This status is read on-device only; it is never transmitted anywhere.

FocusFrame also uses Live Activities to display your active timer on the Lock Screen and Dynamic Island. All Live Activity data (task name, timer progress, session type) is processed entirely on your device and is not shared with any external service.

Ambient Sound Processing

FocusFrame offers ambient sounds (White Noise, Brown Noise, Rain, Ocean Waves) during focus sessions. These sounds are generated or played locally on your device. No audio data is recorded, collected, or transmitted. The app does not access your microphone for this feature.

Children's Privacy

Our apps do not collect data from anyone, including children. Since no personal information is collected, our apps are safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update this page with the new date. Given that our core principle is zero data collection, we do not anticipate significant changes.
