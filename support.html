Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?
All our apps are sold through the App Store. To restore your purchase, simply download the app again from the App Store using the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase. You will not be charged again. Go to App Store → tap your profile → Purchased → find the app → download.
How does iCloud sync work?
Some of our apps (PetLog, FocusFrame, VoiceInk) support iCloud sync. Your data is synced through your own Apple iCloud account. We never see or access your data. To enable or disable sync, go to your device's Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → and toggle the app. Make sure you are signed into the same Apple ID on all devices.
Do your apps work offline?
Yes. All our apps work 100% offline. Every feature runs locally on your device. An internet connection is only needed if you choose to use iCloud sync.
Do you collect any data or analytics?
No. We collect zero data. No analytics, no tracking, no advertising, no third-party SDKs. Your data stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.
Do your apps require an account to use?
No. None of our apps require you to create an account, sign up, or log in. Just download and start using.
Which devices are supported?
All our apps support iPhone and iPad running iOS 17 or later, and Mac running macOS 14 (Sonoma) or later. One purchase works on all your Apple devices (Universal Purchase).
I found a bug or have a feature request.
We would love to hear from you. Please email us at focusframe-support@agentmail.to with a description of the issue or your idea. Include your device model and iOS version if reporting a bug.
Can I get a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple since all purchases are made through the App Store. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund from Apple.
Why are your apps paid instead of free with ads?
We believe you should pay once and own the app forever, with no ads, no subscriptions, and no data harvesting. Your attention and your privacy are worth more than ad revenue. We keep prices low so it is accessible to everyone.
